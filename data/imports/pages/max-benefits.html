Maximizing Resources for Student Success was developed by Johnson County Community College in cooperation with the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, Delta Cost Project at the American Institutes for Research (AIR) and the National Center for Higher Education Management Systems (NCHEMS).

National and Academic Unit Reports

National reports are prepared for each member. Reports include members' values and percentile ranks, and are compared to the 10th, 25th, 50th, 75th, and 90th percentiles of national aggregate data for each benchmark. An institutional report showing the internal comparisons of your academic units is also provided.

Peer Comparisons

Members have access to the Maximizing Resources website to select benchmarks and peer institutions and display reports of benchmark comparisons. Comparisons are available for benchmarks for which institutions submitted data and are reported as rankings. Reporting institutions are identified by name; peer institutions are identified anonymously.

Best Performers

Participating institutions ranking above the 85th percentile on one or more benchmarks will, with their permission, be listed in the Maximizing Resources Best Practices Report Tool (without associated benchmark values).
