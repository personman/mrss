The project captures, analyzes and benchmarks the cost and outcomes of essential instructional and student success activities. Results can be used to allocate resources to activities with the highest beneficial impact on student learning and outcomes.

Maximizing Resources for Student Success provides community colleges with national cost benchmarks on the following activities:

  • Instructional
  • Student services
  • Academic support

The project captures the cost of these activities in relationship to key student outcomes. The results allow institutions to align expenditures with their priorities.

Activity-Based Costing

Most phone book-sized college budgets are too complicated for decision-making and do not provide valid cost information. Activity-based costing offers a clearer picture of all the elements that contribute to the cost of a specific activity.

Maximizing Resources for Student Success provides community colleges with national cost benchmarks on instructional, student services, and academic support activities. Community colleges use the benchmarking for decision making, strategic planning and process improvement.
