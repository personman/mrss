National Reports

National reports are prepared for each member. Reports include members' benchmark values, percentile ranks, and the percentiles of national aggregate data for each benchmark.

Peer Comparisons

Members have unlimited access to the Workforce Project website to select benchmarks and peer institutions and display reports of benchmark comparisons. Comparisons are available for benchmarks for which institutions submitted data and are reported as rankings. Reporting institutions are identified by name; peer institutions are identified anonymously.

Summary Report

This report is prepared just for your college and contains the key benchmarks in the study graphically displayed.  Easy to print, it makes a great report for management.

Membership Directory

Includes information about member institutions' enrollment, service area, and demographics to facilitate selection of comparison groups.
