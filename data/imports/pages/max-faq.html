Data Entry

In the Student Services areas do I include total expenditures for all staff working in the service area, or is reporting limited to professional staff? For example, do I include student employees who help other students with online registration in the Admissions Student Services area?

Report the total expenditures for all staff that are working in Student Services area you are reporting. Yes, include student employees who help other students with online registration toward Admissions..

Are grants and gifts included in financial figures?

Grants and gifts are included if they are utilized for instructional expenses directly related to the activity.

How do I define my service area?

Use the legal definition of service area as defined by your State Higher Education Executive Officer (SHEEO).

For some data elements, we are asked to use a specified fiscal year.  Will that be true for all data elements?

Please use the time period indicated for the data element. Not all data elements have the same time period. If there is no time period indicated please use the contact form and let us know and we will clarify.

What if my Fall IPEDS enrollment information is still preliminary?

Please use the time period indicated for the data element even if that information is still pre-audit.

Is dean oversight allocated into the categories on Form 2: Student Services Activities and Form 3: Academic Support Activities? If so does it include the Assistant to the Dean and secretaries?

Include only those who are directly involved in providing those activities and not those who are only involved in oversight of the activity. Include assistants to those directly involved in providing those activities.

Data Analysis

How do I calculate the % of credit hours taught by full-time?

Enter the total number of credit sections with six or more students taught by full-time faculty at the end of the reporting term divided by the total number of student credit hours taught. Use the total number of student credit hours taught by full-time faculty at the end of the reporting term. This should be the number of credit hours for a course times the number of students enrolled in the course (include students who received a grade of W). For example, a 3 credit hour course with 30 enrolled students would generate 90 student credit hours. (Exclude high school students taking classes taught by high school faculty in their high schools for college credit.)

Scope of Maximizing Resources

Does Maximizing Resources benchmark discipline level data?

No, The Maximizing Resources provides benchmarking data at the institutional level. If you are interested in benchhmarking discipline level data, consider the Cost & Productivity Project, formerly known as "The Kansas Study", provides discipline-level data and benchmarks on instructional costs and workload. Learn about the Cost & Productivity Project.

Does Maximizing Resources include non-credit expenses?

No, Maximizing Resources is collecting data for credit expenses only. If you are interested in non-credit expenses, consider the Workforce Training Benchmark Project that collects data in the areas of non-credit enrollment, retention, revenue, expenditures and more. Learn about the Workforce Training Project.
