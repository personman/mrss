Fred Lillibridge, Associate Vice President for Accreditation, Compliance and Planning
Doña Ana Community College

Using NCCBP Data to Support an HLC Self-Study: Some Examples

Doña Ana Community College, an NCCBP member since 2006, made a strategic and financial decision to stop participating in national surveys to provide data elements to support our HLC study and instead find better ways to use the data supplied in the NCCBP and our internal surveys. This presentation will share examples of how DACC has been able to use this approach to provide effective data support for our study.
