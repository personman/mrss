Changes in the 2014 NCCBP Reports

Graphs: New for the 2014 reports are bar graphs for each benchmark which quickly gives you a snapshot of your college's performance compared to the national benchmarks. Members can either print the graphs or download them as images or PDFs and add them to reports for your college. To print or download the graphs, click on the .

Peer Comparison Tool: The Benchmarking Institute has added a new feature to the peer comparison tool so that you can now save a peer group to use multiple times as you select benchmarks. Also, you will find that the benchmarks are now sorted by form making them easier for you to find.

 

Timeline

October
Enrollment and data collection starts. New and returning community colleges join online and are billed for the membership fee. Website sign-in information is e-mailed to subscribers. Online data collection forms are available in the member section of the website.

June
Participant institutional data are due on June 15. Data are automatically checked for missing and inconsistent information. The data verification process starts. Report that lists inconsistencies is available online for review and update.

July
Verified and updated data are due.

August
New NCCBP reports are available online and the Peer Comparison Tool is populated with most recent data.
