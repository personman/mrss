Changes in the 2015 NCCBP Reports

 

New Benchmarks: "On-time" graduation and two-year transfer rates have been added to the 2015 NCCBP reports.  These new benchmarks are in addition to the standard three and six-year transfer and graduation rates.  
 

Custom Reports: You are now able to create custom reports with NCCBP.  You can build percentile bar graphs, trend graphs, scatter plots and bubble charts.  Additionally, you can select the benchmarks, the data years and the peer institutions to be presented in the graph.

 

Timeline

October
Enrollment and data collection starts. New and returning community colleges join online and are billed for the membership fee. Website sign-in information is e-mailed to subscribers. Online data collection forms are available in the member section of the website.

June
Participant institutional data are due on June 15. Data are automatically checked for missing and inconsistent information. The data verification process starts. Report that lists inconsistencies is available online for review and update.

July
Verified and updated data are due.

August
New NCCBP reports are available online and the Peer Comparison Tool is populated with most recent data.
