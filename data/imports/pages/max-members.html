Data Entry

 

Data Entry Requirements

We encourage every participating institution to submit as many data elements as possible. If you do not have data for a particular data element, you may leave that cell blank. However, the more data you provide, the better you will be able to benchmark your institution.

The only mandatory data elements are located on Form 5 (highlighted by asterisks). Please contact us if you are not able to provide one of the mandatory data elements.

Finalizing Your Data

You do not need to lock or submit your data. We will automatically lock your account after the data verification process has been completed.

As this is the first year of Maximizing Resources the data deadlines may be subject to extension to allow all members adequate time to participate.
