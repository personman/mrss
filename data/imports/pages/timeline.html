October

Join or renew membership
Annual enrollment begins. New and returning community colleges join online.

June

Finish data collection
Member institutional data are due on June 15. Data are automatically checked for missing and inconsistent information. The data verification process starts. Report that lists inconsistencies is available online for review and update.

July

Verify your data
Verified and updated data are due to the NCCBP. So that NCCBP results can be included in participants' planning and reporting processes, timely corrections and updates are expected.

September

Reports are available
NCCBP reports of aggregate results that compare member institutions' data to national benchmark values are available, the Peer Comparison Tool is populated with most recent data.NCCBP and the Best Performers is issued.
